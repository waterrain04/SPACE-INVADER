Space Invaders is a classic arcade video game released in 1978 by Taito. The game's objective is to control a small spaceship at the bottom of the screen and shoot at rows of descending aliens. As the game progresses, the aliens move faster and become more numerous, increasing the game's difficulty. Space Invaders is credited with popularizing the "shoot 'em up" genre of video games, and has had a significant impact on popular culture.

Space Invaders was an instant hit in arcades around the world and spawned numerous sequels and imitators. It is considered one of the most important and influential video games of all time and is credited with popularizing video games as a mainstream form of entertainment.
